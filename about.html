About

Medtracker is a web app produced by 4 first year students at The University of Manchester for the Sheffield hackathon "HackMed 2018". It provides users with reminders for their medicine and allows prescription personalisation.

Why Medtracker?

Medtracker displays all of the information needed for medicine and their administration. It uses the Nexmo API to send reminders via phone text message.






Developed by Team Kilburners, The University of Manchester
